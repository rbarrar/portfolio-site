About Me

I am an up and coming Front-End Web Developer with a passion for Marketing. I received my bachelors degree in marketing from the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University.

Following my undergraduate education I attended and completed a 9-week coding bootcamp at Wyncode Academy . Throughout my time at Wyncode we were taught the following languages and Frameworks:

  • Ruby
  • Ruby on Rails
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • jQuery

Since completing Wyncode I have been working hard to continue my learning. I have taken a variety of courses online that have a covered topics such as JavaScript sorting algorithms, Responsive Web Design and more. Additionally I am currently enrolled in Wyncode's continuing education program Wynmasters, where we have dived deeper into Ruby on Rails and covered topics such as performance tesing, r-spec testing, and even building our own framework.

Although coding is a major part of my life my other hobbies include; watching the NBA, catching up on the latest Game of Thrones (episodes and fan theories), listen to the latest Future and Chance the Rapper projects, or enjoying a craft beer somewhere around Miami.
